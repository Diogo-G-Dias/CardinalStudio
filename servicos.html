Common questions

If yours isn't here, just ask — via the form, WhatsApp, or the assistant directly.

How much?
No fixed public price — the right site for you depends on what's eating time now. We talk first, I see what's worth building, and I quote with 'friendly pricing' as a principle. Pro tier is typically 4-6 weeks of work. No vendor-promised sales numbers.
Isn't it cheaper to DIY on Wix?
Yes, it is. If your budget is zero and time is plenty, build on Wix — I'll show you the shortcuts. If you want a chatbot booking meetings while you sleep, or integrations that scale, Wix gives you half. Cardinal Studio fills that gap — when DIY stops being enough.
Can I trust a chatbot to talk to my clients?
Clear AI disclosure before every conversation. Transcripts kept with controlled retention — I see them, no one else. When the assistant can't handle something, it forwards to your WhatsApp or email. Not AI dressed up as human — a clear tool with a human backup.
How long?
Pro tier: 4-6 weeks, depending on content delivery (copy, photos) and how fast you respond. No guaranteed quick turnarounds — I prefer dates I can hit. If you have a fair or opening, mention it on the first call.
Who maintains the site after?
Me. The same Diogo who built it. Not handed off to any rotating agency, nor to next-cycle freelancers. It's one of the practical reasons not to split vendors: the person who built it knows why every piece is where it is.
