I'm Diogo.

I work out of Alentejo, Portugal. I build sites for small businesses and teach them to handle operational work on their own — answering calls, booking meetings, qualifying leads. Cardinal Studio is the name for that single piece.

Before this, I did web development, product design, project management, and game dev. Not a résumé — it's the reason you get one person instead of three. When your site has to learn to book meetings, the same head is on it.

I'm doing this now because AI finally made it possible to give a small business operational capabilities once reserved for large companies. The tools exist — what's missing is someone close to you who can wire them together, with no agency in between.

Web, AI and calendar wired by the same person. No coordinating between three specialists who don't talk to each other.

How I work

It starts with a conversation. Before any proposal, any price, any line of code.

  1. Talk first

    A free call. We map what eats your day — missed calls, manual bookings, email back-and-forth — before I propose anything.

  2. Clear proposal

    Proposal in plain language. Friendly pricing up front, honest timelines, and what is and isn't included. No 'custom quotes'.

  3. Site first

    I ship the site first. Then we wire the pieces — chatbot, calendar, integrations — as you feel where it hurts most.

  4. I stay close

    Not a build-and-disappear project. I stay available after launch to tune, expand and fix. Same person, always.
